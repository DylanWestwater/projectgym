Our Mission!

Here at project gym we know how confusing it is trying to research facts about the gym. Whether your aim is to lose fat, gain muscle or both, the internet is crawling with people who are offering the next best four week plan to get you to look like a Greek god or goddess. We prefer to take a different approach, with simple, easy to understand exercises, you can spend less time googling and more time lifting!

Back Exercises

Pull Ups

Pull ups are done by grabbing a bar above head height and lifting yourself up until your chin clears the bar.

Rows

Rows are completed by grabbing a weight infront of the body and pulling it back towards the chest.

Lat Pulldown

Lat pulldowns are performed by grabbing a weight above the head and pulling it down towards the chest.
